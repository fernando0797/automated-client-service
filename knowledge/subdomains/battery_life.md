## Description
Issues where a device's battery drains faster than expected, does not hold charge, or behaves inconsistently.

## Common Issues
- Rapid battery drain
- Device shutting down unexpectedly
- Battery not charging fully
- Overheating during use
- Sudden battery percentage drops

## Root Causes
- Background apps consuming power
- Software bugs or recent updates
- High brightness or performance settings
- Battery aging or degradation
- Faulty charging cables or adapters
- Environmental conditions (heat/cold)

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Reduce screen brightness
2. Close background apps
3. Disable unnecessary features (Bluetooth, GPS)
4. Update device software
5. Restart device
6. Check battery usage statistics
7. Use original charger
8. Perform battery calibration if applicable

## When to Escalate
- Battery drains extremely fast (< few hours)
- Device overheats consistently
- Physical battery swelling
- Charging not working at all

## Response Guidelines
- Ask about usage patterns
- Suggest optimizations before hardware conclusions
- Provide safe charging practices
- Avoid recommending risky actions
