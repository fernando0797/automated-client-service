# Hardware Issue

## Description
Problems related to physical components of a device.

## Common Issues
- Device not turning on
- Broken components
- Ports not working
- Unusual noises
- Overheating

## Root Causes
- Physical damage
- Wear and tear
- Manufacturing defects
- Power issues
- Environmental conditions

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Check power supply
2. Inspect for visible damage
3. Restart device
4. Disconnect peripherals
5. Test with alternative components

## When to Escalate
- Device not powering on
- Burning smell or overheating
- Critical component failure

## Response Guidelines
- Prioritize safety
- Avoid risky instructions
- Recommend official repair channels
