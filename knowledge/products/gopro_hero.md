# GoPro Hero

## Overview
The GoPro Hero is a compact action camera designed for capturing high-quality video and images in extreme conditions. It is widely used for sports, outdoor activities, and content creation.

## Key Features
- 4K video recording (depending on model)
- Waterproof design (typically up to 10m without housing)
- Wide-angle lens
- Image stabilization (HyperSmooth in newer models)
- Voice control
- Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity

## Common Issues
- Battery drains quickly during recording
- Overheating during long recordings
- SD card errors
- Device freezing or not responding
- Connectivity issues with mobile app

## Technical Specifications
- Storage: microSD (Class 10 or UHS-I recommended)
- Battery: Lithium-ion rechargeable
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth
- File formats: MP4 (H.264 / H.265)

## Troubleshooting Guide
### Battery Issues
- Reduce resolution/frame rate
- Turn off Wi-Fi when not needed
- Use official batteries

### Overheating
- Avoid direct sunlight
- Limit long recordings
- Ensure firmware is updated

### SD Card Errors
- Format SD card in device
- Use recommended cards
- Replace faulty card

### Freezing
- Force restart (hold power button)
- Update firmware

## Compatibility
- Compatible with GoPro mobile app
- Supports various mounts and accessories

## Best Practices
- Use high-speed SD cards
- Keep firmware updated
- Avoid fully draining battery frequently
