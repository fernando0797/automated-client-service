## Overview
GoPro action cameras are rugged, compact cameras designed for capturing high-quality video and images in extreme environments such as sports, underwater, and outdoor activities.

## Key Features
- 4K/5K video recording (model dependent)
- Waterproof and shock-resistant design
- Advanced image stabilization
- Wide-angle lens
- Voice control and mobile app integration

## Common Issues
- Overheating during recording
- Battery draining quickly
- SD card errors
- Camera freezing
- Connectivity issues with app

## Technical Specifications
- Storage: microSD (UHS-I/UHS-II recommended)
- Battery: Rechargeable lithium-ion
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth

## Troubleshooting Guide
### Overheating
- Reduce resolution/frame rate
- Avoid direct sunlight
- Limit continuous recording time

### SD Card Issues
- Format card in device
- Use recommended cards
- Replace damaged cards

### Connectivity Issues
- Reset wireless connections
- Update firmware
- Reinstall app

## Compatibility
- Works with GoPro mobile app
- Supports wide range of mounts and accessories

## Best Practices
- Use certified SD cards
- Keep firmware updated
- Avoid long recordings without breaks
