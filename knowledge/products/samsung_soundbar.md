# Samsung Soundbar

## Overview
Samsung Soundbars are external audio systems designed to enhance TV sound quality, offering immersive audio experiences.

## Key Features
- Surround sound support
- Wireless subwoofer (model dependent)
- HDMI ARC/eARC connectivity
- Bluetooth streaming
- Dolby Atmos support (high-end models)

## Common Issues
- No sound output
- Audio delay (lip sync issues)
- Bluetooth pairing problems
- Subwoofer not connecting
- Remote not responding

## Technical Specifications
- Connectivity: HDMI, Bluetooth, Optical
- Power: Electric
- Audio formats: Dolby, DTS

## Troubleshooting Guide
### No Sound
- Check input source
- Verify cable connections
- Adjust TV audio settings

### Audio Delay
- Adjust audio sync settings
- Use HDMI ARC connection

### Connectivity Issues
- Reset Bluetooth
- Re-pair devices
- Restart soundbar

## Compatibility
- Compatible with TVs, smartphones, consoles
- Works best with Samsung TVs

## Best Practices
- Use HDMI ARC for best quality
- Keep firmware updated
- Avoid signal interference
