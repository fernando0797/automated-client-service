## Overview
Canon DSLR cameras are digital single-lens reflex cameras designed for photography enthusiasts and professionals, offering manual controls and interchangeable lenses.

## Key Features
- Interchangeable lenses
- Optical viewfinder
- High-resolution sensors
- Manual and automatic shooting modes
- RAW image support

## Common Issues
- Camera not turning on
- Autofocus not working
- Blurry images
- SD card errors
- Battery draining quickly

## Technical Specifications
- Sensor: APS-C or Full Frame (depending on model)
- Storage: SD/SDHC/SDXC cards
- Battery: Rechargeable lithium-ion

## Troubleshooting Guide
### Power Issues
- Check battery charge
- Clean battery contacts
- Use original battery

### Focus Issues
- Check autofocus settings
- Clean lens
- Switch focus modes

### SD Card Issues
- Format card in camera
- Use recommended cards
- Replace faulty card

## Compatibility
- Compatible with Canon EF/EF-S lenses
- Supports external flashes and accessories

## Best Practices
- Keep sensor and lens clean
- Use protective cases
- Update firmware
