# Bose QuietComfort

## Overview
Bose QuietComfort is a premium line of wireless noise-cancelling headphones designed for high-quality audio and comfort, especially during travel and long listening sessions.

## Key Features
- Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)
- Bluetooth connectivity
- Long battery life (up to ~24 hours)
- Comfortable over-ear design
- Multipoint device connection

## Common Issues
- Bluetooth connection drops
- Noise cancellation not working properly
- Battery draining faster than expected
- Audio imbalance
- Headphones not turning on

## Technical Specifications
- Connectivity: Bluetooth
- Battery: Rechargeable lithium-ion
- Charging: USB-C (newer models)
- Controls: Physical buttons or touch (depending on model)

## Troubleshooting Guide
### Connection Issues
- Reset Bluetooth connection
- Forget and re-pair device
- Update firmware via Bose app

### ANC Issues
- Check ANC settings in app
- Restart headphones
- Ensure proper fit

### Battery Issues
- Fully charge device
- Avoid extreme temperatures
- Replace battery if degraded (if applicable)

## Compatibility
- Compatible with iOS, Android, Windows
- Works with Bose Music app

## Best Practices
- Keep firmware updated
- Store in case when not in use
- Avoid moisture exposure
