# Fitbit Charge

## Overview
Fitbit Charge is a fitness tracker designed to monitor health metrics such as heart rate, steps, sleep, and activity levels.

## Key Features
- Heart rate monitoring
- Sleep tracking
- Activity tracking (steps, calories, distance)
- GPS (model dependent)
- Smartphone notifications

## Common Issues
- Battery draining quickly
- Sync issues with app
- Heart rate sensor not accurate
- Device not charging
- Screen not responding

## Technical Specifications
- Connectivity: Bluetooth
- Battery life: ~5–7 days
- App: Fitbit app (iOS/Android)

## Troubleshooting Guide
### Sync Issues
- Restart device and phone
- Reinstall Fitbit app
- Toggle Bluetooth

### Battery Issues
- Reduce screen brightness
- Disable continuous tracking features
- Update firmware

### Charging Issues
- Clean charging contacts
- Use official charger

## Compatibility
- Compatible with iOS and Android devices
- Integrates with health apps

## Best Practices
- Keep firmware updated
- Clean device regularly
- Charge before full depletion
