## Overview
ASUS ROG (Republic of Gamers) is a line of high-performance gaming laptops and hardware designed for intensive workloads and gaming.

## Key Features
- High-end GPUs (NVIDIA RTX series)
- High refresh rate displays
- Advanced cooling systems
- RGB customization
- Gaming optimization software

## Common Issues
- Overheating
- Fan noise
- Battery drain
- Performance drops
- Driver conflicts

## Technical Specifications
- OS: Windows
- GPU: Dedicated (NVIDIA/AMD)
- RAM: High capacity (16GB+ recommended)
- Storage: SSD

## Troubleshooting Guide
### Overheating
- Clean fans
- Use cooling pad
- Adjust performance settings

### Performance Issues
- Update drivers
- Close background apps
- Optimize power settings

### Battery Issues
- Use plugged mode for gaming
- Calibrate battery

## Compatibility
- Compatible with gaming peripherals
- Supports external monitors

## Best Practices
- Keep drivers updated
- Maintain cooling system
- Avoid prolonged overheating
