# Garmin Forerunner

## Overview
Garmin Forerunner is a GPS smartwatch designed for runners and athletes, offering advanced performance tracking and training metrics.

## Key Features
- GPS tracking
- Heart rate monitoring
- Training metrics and VO2 max
- Long battery life
- Garmin Connect integration

## Common Issues
- GPS not accurate
- Battery draining quickly
- Sync issues with app
- Heart rate inaccuracies
- Device freezing

## Technical Specifications
- Connectivity: Bluetooth, ANT+
- Battery life: varies (up to ~2 weeks smartwatch mode)
- App: Garmin Connect

## Troubleshooting Guide
### GPS Issues
- Wait for full satellite lock
- Update firmware
- Use open sky environment

### Battery Issues
- Disable unnecessary sensors
- Reduce backlight usage

### Sync Issues
- Restart device
- Reconnect app

## Compatibility
- Compatible with iOS/Android
- Integrates with fitness platforms

## Best Practices
- Sync regularly
- Update firmware
- Use correct activity modes
