## Overview
Bose SoundLink speakers are portable Bluetooth speakers known for high-quality audio, durability, and ease of use.

## Key Features
- Wireless Bluetooth streaming
- Portable design
- Rechargeable battery
- Water-resistant (depending on model)
- Built-in microphone for calls

## Common Issues
- Bluetooth pairing issues
- Speaker not turning on
- Battery not charging
- Distorted sound
- Intermittent audio

## Technical Specifications
- Connectivity: Bluetooth
- Battery: Rechargeable lithium-ion
- Range: ~9 meters (30 feet)

## Troubleshooting Guide
### Pairing Issues
- Reset Bluetooth connection
- Clear paired devices list
- Restart speaker

### Battery Issues
- Use original charger
- Check charging port
- Replace battery if needed

### Sound Issues
- Reduce interference
- Move closer to source device
- Restart speaker

## Compatibility
- Compatible with smartphones, tablets, laptops
- Works with Bose app (model dependent)

## Best Practices
- Keep firmware updated
- Avoid full battery depletion
- Store in dry conditions
